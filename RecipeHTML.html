SAMSON'S GUACAMOLE RECIPE!

Ingredients

  • 5 avocados
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Cayenne Powder(optional)
  • 1/4 wedge of lime

Instructions

  1. Skin all the avocados into a large mixing bowl
  2. Use a fork to break avocados into smaller chunks
  3. Apply salt, pepper, chopped cilantro and onion to your own preference
  4. Add Cayenne at your own discretion
  5. Squeeze 1/4 wedge of lime juice directly into bowl
  6. Use fork and mix until you get a chunky consistency

  7. BONNE APPETIT!
